Help & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new inventory item?

Navigate to the Inventory Management page, fill in all required fields, and click the "Add Item" button.

How do I edit an existing item?

Enter the item name in the form on the Inventory Management page, make the necessary changes, and click the "Edit Item" button.

How do I delete an item?

Enter the item name in the delete section on the Inventory Management page and click the "Delete Item" button. You will be prompted to confirm the deletion.

How do I search for items?

Navigate to the Search page, enter the item name in the search box, and click the "Search" button.

How do I view popular items?

Navigate to the Search page and click the "Display Popular Items" button.

Troubleshooting

Item not found when editing

Ensure you have entered the correct item name. Check the inventory list to confirm the exact name of the item.

ID already exists error

Each item must have a unique ID. Please choose a different ID for the item you are trying to add or edit.

Invalid input error

Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly. Numeric fields (quantity, price) should only contain numbers.
